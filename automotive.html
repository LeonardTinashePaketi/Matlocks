Modern Vehicle Security Experts

At Matlocks, we understand specifically how stressful it is to be locked out of your vehicle or deal with a broken ignition. Our automotive locksmith services are designed to be fast, non-destructive, and comprehensive. We handle everything from classic cars to modern keyless entry systems.

Car Door Lock Repairs

Whether your lock is jammed due to wear and tear or attempted theft, our technicians can restore it to perfect working condition without damaging your vehicle's paint or structure.

Key Fob Replacement & Programming

Lost your remote? We stock a wide range of transponder keys and remotes. We program them on-site to match your car's immobilizer system, often at a fraction of the dealership cost.

Keyless Entry & Smart Systems

Troubleshooting proximity sensors and smart entry push-to-start systems. We ensure your advanced security features are functioning correctly so you never lose access.
